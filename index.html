Last updated: July 7, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Chicken Catch ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Chicken Catch mobile application.

Chicken Catch is a fully offline casual arcade game where players move a basket to catch falling eggs, earn points, and achieve their highest score. All gameplay takes place locally on the user's device.

How Chicken Catch Works

Players control a basket to catch eggs dropped by chickens. Each caught egg increases the score, while missed eggs reduce the available lives. The objective is to beat your personal best score.

The entire game works offline and does not require an internet connection.

Local Data Storage

Chicken Catch may store only basic game information locally on your device, such as your highest score and simple game preferences.

This information never leaves your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Internet Access

Chicken Catch does not require internet access to function.

Every feature is available completely offline.

Permissions

Chicken Catch does not request any Android permissions.

The application does not access the camera, microphone, contacts, location, storage, SMS messages, phone information, or any sensitive device features.

Third-Party Services

Chicken Catch does not use:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics platforms
  • Firebase
  • Tracking SDKs
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud services
  • External APIs

Children's Privacy

Chicken Catch does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal information is collected, the game is suitable for players of all ages.

Data Security

Because all gameplay and saved information remain on your own device, users maintain full control over their local game data.

Your Rights

Since Chicken Catch does not collect or process personal information, there is no personal data for us to access, modify, export, or delete.

Users can remove all locally stored game data at any time by clearing the application's storage or uninstalling the application.
